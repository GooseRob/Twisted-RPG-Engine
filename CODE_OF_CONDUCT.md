# Code of Conduct

This project is dedicated to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful
environment for all contributors, users, and community members.

---

# Our Standards

We pledge to:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Welcome diverse viewpoints
- Provide constructive feedback
- Avoid harassment or discrimination
- Maintain a positive, collaborative environment

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without consent
- Spamming or advertising unrelated content

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers may:

- Warn contributors
- Remove inappropriate content
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors
- Close or lock discussions

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

- Open an issue (if appropriate)
- Or contact the maintainers privately

All reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly.

---

# Commitment

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.
